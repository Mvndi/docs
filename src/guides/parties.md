# Parties

To use it, `/party invite <player>` to your party. They will receive a message in their chat. Click it to join the party.
As long as all party members are within a **100 block radius** of the party owner. Then there is a **50/25% chance (respectively)** that EXP and profession progress will be distributed to ALL ONLINE party members (in the radius, homogeneous professions only). Note that the maximum party member count is **16**.

To disband a party `/party disband`
To leave a party `/party leave`
To kick a member `/party kick <player>`
To promote a member to party leader `/party promote <player>`
To check party stats `/party stats`. Your username will be colored YELLOW if you are out of range.
To check your individual party stats `/party mystats`
