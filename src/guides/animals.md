# Animals

All Minecraft animals that were in the map area during the Middle Ages can be found somewhere on the map.

Animals spawn in IRL locations.
- Cows, sheeps, pigs, chickens & rabbits spawn almost everywhere with various amounts.
- Horses spawn mostly in steppe or plains.
- Donkeys spawn in temperate biomes.
- Camels spawn in oasis, Nile valley, Sahara & arabian desert.
- Frogs spawn in swamp biome.
- Parrots spawn in warm atlantic island only.
- Cats spawn in Nile valley.
- Foxes spawn in cold biomes.
- Goats spawn in mountains.
- Turtules spawn in warm beach.
- Salmons spawn in cold & temperate ocean & river.
- Cods & dolphins spawn in temperate & warm ocean.
- Tropical fishes & pufferfishes spawn in warm ocean.
- Glow squids spawn in swamp caves.

Some animal's colors are linked to some biomes, such as white fox spawn in snowy biome & gold rabbits spawns in deserts.
