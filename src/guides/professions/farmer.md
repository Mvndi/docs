# Farmer (GUIDE WIP)

## XP Gain

Farmer profession gains XP from breaking fully harvested crops such as wheat, cabbage, carrots, etc.

## Skill Tree

Most of these are self explanatory so only explaining the not intuitive skills (wip guide will rewrite later)

Seedbags. WIP Guide I will look at the specifics later but seedbags are basically bundles (they were made before bundles) but you put them into crafting table with seeds.

If you have flax skill you break normal foliage to get flax seeds. Similar with rosemary and sage just break foliage if you have the skill to get the seeds, you can also break flax (the blue) flowers with a scythe to get flax seeds. Right click grass to plant them.

Windmills speed up growth of crops within 3x3 chunk radius

Hold shift and right click tamed donkey to attach the plough. Can only plough in claim chunks of your town. It has to be a donkey that you tamed for you to be able to attach the plough.

## Machines

Machines need the preindustrial skill from farming tree. They need to be reconnected (right click with wrench to connect it) to their generator (windmill) every server restart.
