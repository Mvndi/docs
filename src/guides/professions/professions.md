# Professions (GUIDE WIP)

Can be accessed from `/mp` or `/professions`

You can join 2 professions max but if you join 2 then you can only complete half of each tree (or 1/4 one tree and 3/4 another). If you're in just 1 profession then you can complete the whole tree.

Various resources and crafting recipes are locked behind professions such as needing a blacksmith to refine raw iron into steel or cast iron or needing a farmer to get machines like the mill to make stuff like bread.

Profession XP is shown in the hud to the left of your hotbar, it's seperate than attribute XP.
