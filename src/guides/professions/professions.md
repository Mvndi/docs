# Professions

Can be accessed from `/mp` or `/professions`

You can join 2 professions and complete the whole tree of each profession.

Various resources and crafting recipes are locked behind professions such as needing a blacksmith to refine raw iron into steel or cast iron or needing a farmer to get machines like the mill to make stuff like bread.

Profession XP is shown in the hud to the left of your hotbar, it's seperate than attribute XP. If you want the exact xp use `/mu stats`.

Each profession level require a bit more xp than the previous level.
