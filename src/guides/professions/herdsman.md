# Herdsman (GUIDE WIP)

## XP Gain

There are a couple of ways to gain XP for the herdsman profession:

1. Breeding animals, for horses specifically you can use hay bales and the horses must be tamed
2. Hunting mobs like cows sheep and pigs
3. Sheering sheep

## Skill Tree

Most of these are self explanatory so only explaining the not intuitive skills (wip guide will rewrite later)

To use the trowel to get worms you have to right click grass blocks with the trowel while it's raining. Hold worm in offhand when fishing with fishing rod, there is a random chance to start a minigame where you will see a bar appear at the top of your screen, you have to right click and fill the bar in a certain amount of time to win the minigame and receive a good fish.

Shepehrds staff makes sheep follow you when you hold it.

To start the truffle minigame you have to leash a pig then it will go in the direction of truffles you have to find them by right clicking the ground. Eating truffle gives speed for 10s.

You have random chance to get barely seeds when breaking wheat if you have the skill.

Hold shift and right click tamed donkey/horse to attach the cart.  It has to be a saddled donkey/horse that you tamed for you to be able to attach the cart and you need the skill.

To open horse genetics menu you need to have the relevant skill in herdsman tree and you have to shift right click a baby with an empty hand. Most the stats are self explanetory but bravery decreases the chance for the horse to rear when taking damage.

- Feeding baby horse apples gives health
- Feeding baby horse barley gives jump
- Feeding baby horse sugar gives speed
- Feeding baby horse horse hay bale blocks gives bravery

To work with cow/sheep/chicken genetics they have to be bred with barley, you shift right click them to open the genetics menu, doesn't have to be a baby. The temperature where the cows are bred at determines their genetics and there's also some rng.
