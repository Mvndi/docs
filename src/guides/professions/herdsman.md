# Herdsman (GUIDE WIP)

## XP Gain

There are a couple of ways to gain XP for the herdsman profession:

1. Breeding animals, for horses specifically you can use hay bales and the horses must be tamed
2. Hunting mobs like cows sheep and pigs
3. Sheering sheep

## Skill Tree

Most of these are self explanatory so only explaining the not intuitive skills (wip guide will rewrite later)

To use the trowel to get worms you have to right click grass blocks with the trowel while it's raining. Hold worm in offhand when fishing with fishing rod to start the minigame (will write minigame specifics later...)

Shepehrds staff makes sheep follow you when you hold it.

To start the truffle minigame you have to walk a pig around on a lead and it will spawn truffles you have to find by right clicking the ground

Cheese crafting is you put glass bottle and milk or something into crafting table have to look at this later to write the specifics.

Hold shift and right click tamed donkey/horse to attach the cart.  It has to be a donkey/horse that you tamed for you to be able to attach the cart.
