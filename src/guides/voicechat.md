# Voice Chat

The simple voice chat mod is supported. It is NOT required, but highly recommended for immersion purposes :)

## How to Download

Simple download the mod from [here](https://modrinth.com/plugin/simple-voice-chat/) and put it in your mods folder.
Then, join a server or single player world and press 'V' to setup your microphone and other settings.
When speaking, a microphone icon the bottom left corner of your screen should indicate that the mod is working properly.
