# Towny Upkeep

- town upkeep is `$30 * number of plots * upkeep multiplier`
- nation upkeep is `$250 *  number of plots * upkeep multiplier`

upkeep multiplier is based off town/nation level (which is based off number of residents/towns) as seen in the tables below

(town/nation levels are used for other stuff too like prefixes, suffixes, and how many offensive sieges you're allowed to have)

## Town Upkeep Multipliers

| Town Level | Town Residents | Upkeep Multiplier |
|------------|----------------|-------------------|
| 1          | 0              | 1.0               |
| 2          | 1              | 1.0               |
| 3          | 2              | 1.0               |
| 4          | 5              | 1.0               |
| 5          | 10             | 1.1               |
| 6          | 15             | 1.2               |
| 7          | 20             | 1.3               |
| 8          | 25             | 1.4               |
| 9          | 30             | 1.5               |

## Nation Upkeep Multipliers

| Nation Level | Number of Towns | Upkeep Multiplier |
|--------------|-----------------|-------------------|
| 1            | 0               | 1.0               |
| 2            | 2               | 1.5               |
| 3            | 5               | 2.5               |
| 4            | 10              | 3.5               |
| 5            | 15              | 4.0               |
| 6            | 20              | 5.0               |
| 7            | 30              | 8.0               |
